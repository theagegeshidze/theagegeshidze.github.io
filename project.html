Nuclear Power v Renewables: Is there space for nuclear power in the fight against climate change?

Aim: Climate change is one of the most pressing challenges facing our world today, and finding ways to reduce our carbon emissions and protect the planet for future generations is more important than ever. One potential solution that has been proposed is the use of nuclear power as a low-carbon source of electricity. However, the role of nuclear power in the fight against climate change is a topic of ongoing debate, with both supporters and critics raising valid points. Climate activists tend to agree that renewables will play an important role, but the role of nuclear power remains contentious. By comparing the relative; safety, capacity, inertia, cost, stability and possibility of the two options, we aim to explore whether there is space for nuclear power in our fight against the climate crisis.
